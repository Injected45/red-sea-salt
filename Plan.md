# Red Sea Salt — Website Structure & Discovery Questions

## Goal

Create a modern, multi-page corporate website for **Red Sea Salt** that presents the company professionally, supports commercial inquiries, and includes:

* Technical SEO review and adjustments as needed
* On-page SEO refinements for key commercial pages
* Modern, clean, and industry-appropriate UI/UX
* Arabic-first experience, with English support if needed

## Discovery Questions Before Writing the Full MD Plan

### Business & Goals

1. What is the main goal of the website: lead generation, brand presentation, or direct sales inquiries?
2. Is the target market local only, or do you also serve other regions/countries?
3. Do you want the website in Arabic only, or Arabic + English?

### Products

4. Which products should appear on the homepage as the main featured products?
5. Do you have product specifications, packaging sizes, purity percentages, or certifications for each product?
6. Do you want separate SEO pages for each product or one combined products page?

### Branding

7. Do you already have a logo, brand colors, and fonts?
8. Do you want the design style to feel more industrial, premium, minimal, or mixed?

### Content

9. Do you have company photos, factory photos, warehouse photos, or product images?
10. Do you want us to write the website copy from scratch or refine existing content?

### SEO

11. Do you have target keywords in mind, or should they be researched and proposed?
12. Which commercial pages are the highest priority for SEO?
13. Do you want a blog section from launch, or later in phase 2?

### Technical

14. What platform will the site be built on: Laravel, WordPress, custom HTML, or something else?
15. Do you need a contact form connected to email, WhatsApp, or CRM?
16. Do you need bilingual URL structure for Arabic and English pages?

### Operations

17. Who will update products and content after launch?
18. Do you want a quote request form with file upload?
19. Do you need a map, branch details, or export contact info displayed prominently?
